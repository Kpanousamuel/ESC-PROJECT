About ESC Building

The Entrepreneurship Center (ESC) of Ekiti State University serves as a vital platform designed to equip students with practical, skill-based training alongside their academic studies. It is a structured initiative that becomes particularly significant at the 300 level, where students are required to select a specific skill area for specialization.

The primary objective of the ESC is to foster entrepreneurial development, self-reliance, and innovation among students. In recognition of the evolving demands of the modern economy, the center provides a wide range of vocational and technical skill options. These include Animal Production, Carpentry, Catering and Cinematography, Cosmetology and Event Design, Crop Production, Fashion Design for both male and female wear, Hairdressing, Make-up and Millinery, Photography, Solar Installation and Tie and Dye, as well as Web Design.

Through these diverse skill offerings, the ESC ensures that students are not only academically sound but also practically empowered. The center emphasizes hands-on training, creativity, and the development of marketable skills that can serve as a source of income both during and after university education.

In conclusion, the Entrepreneurship Center in Ekiti State University plays a crucial role in bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. It prepares students to become job creators rather than job seekers, thereby contributing positively to personal development and national economic growth.
